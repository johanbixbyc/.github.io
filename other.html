About

My name is Johan Christensen, and I'm a writer based in Seattle.

I hold an MA in philosophy from the University of Chicago, where I taught essay writing and argument structure to first-year students, before returning home to Washington at the start of the pandemic.

I have strong interests in many things, and work hard to continue to learn, both new skills and about the world in general. Currently in my personal time I'm working on a videogame while learning C#, and learning to play tennis.

I am deeply passionate about helping people, whether that be through teaching, activism, or volunteer work. I always strive to better myself through consciously critiquing my own actions, intuitions, and biases, so as to grow and become the best member of my community that I can be.

In addition to my more serious projects, I like to make short films with my friends, make all sorts of music, and find new parks and trails in my area that I haven't visited yet.
